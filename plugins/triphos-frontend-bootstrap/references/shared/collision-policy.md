# Collision Policy

- Keep bundled policy skills namespaced with the `triphos-` prefix.
- Do not overwrite or shadow the user's global skill directories.
- Preserve familiar public aliases in descriptions and router guidance:
  - `fsd-refactor`
  - `react-lint-rules`
  - `api-client-setup`
  - `fsd-update`
  - `fsd-skill-update`
- Treat `fsd-update` and `fsd-skill-update` as the same alias surface for `triphos-fsd-skill-update`.
